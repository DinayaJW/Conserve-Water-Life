Conserve Water Life

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development and protecting the natural beauty and resources found beneath the ocean's surface.


Our Vision

To save Coral reefs, clear waters, marine protected areas, sustainable fishing practices, and thriving coastal communities are all vital for the health and resilience of fish populations.


Our Mission

  1. Implement coral gardening, establish marine protected areas, reduce pollution, promote sustainable tourism, and conduct public awareness campaigns to protect coral reefs.
  2. Implement waste management systems in coastal cities, promote biodegradable materials, reduce single-use plastics, conduct regular beach and underwater clean-ups, strengthen international regulations, and support marine pollution research.
  3. Implement science-based fishing quotas, promote selective gear, support small-scale fisheries, enhance international cooperation against IUCU, and develop sustainable aquaculture practices for environmental and social equity.
  4. The global network of marine protected areas, habitat restoration projects, legal frameworks, community-based conservation initiatives, and international agreements are being implemented to protect endangered marine species.
